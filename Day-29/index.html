What It Is

More on Amazon

Amazon is one of the world’s largest e-commerce companies, originally founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos in Seattle, USA. It began as an online bookstore and quickly expanded into selling almost every kind of product imaginable — from electronics and clothing to groceries and digital goods.

Flipkart

What It Is

More on Flipkart

Flipkart is an Indian e-commerce company headquartered in Bengaluru, Karnataka. It was founded in 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal (both former Amazon employees).

Myntra

What It Is

More on Myntra

Myntra is an Indian fashion and lifestyle e-commerce platform, known for selling clothing, footwear, beauty, and accessories. It was founded in 2007 by Mukesh Bansal, Ashutosh Lawania, and Vineet Saxena.

Online shopping means buying goods or services over the internet using websites or mobile apps — instead of visiting a physical store. Customers can browse products, compare prices, place orders, and pay digitally — all from home
